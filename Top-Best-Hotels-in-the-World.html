Raffles Hotel Singapore is a luxury hotel located in the central business district of Singapore. The hotel was first opened in 1887 and has since become an iconic landmark of Singapore. The hotel features 115 suites and 9 restaurants and bars, including the famous Long Bar where the Singapore Sling cocktail was first created. Other notable features of the hotel include a rooftop swimming pool, a spa, a fitness center, and several event spaces for meetings and weddings. Raffles Hotel has hosted many famous guests over the years, including Queen Elizabeth II, Michael Jackson, and Charlie Chaplin. The hotel has also been featured in several films and novels, such as "Crazy Rich Asians" and "The Raffles Hotel Cookbook." In 2019, Raffles Hotel underwent a major renovation and restoration, which aimed to preserve the heritage of the hotel while upgrading its facilities and services to meet modern expectations.
