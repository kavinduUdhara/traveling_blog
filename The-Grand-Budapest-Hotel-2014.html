The Grand Budapest Hotel (2014)
Ladini Wijerathna
04/02/2023

One potential movie that comes to mind is "The Grand Budapest Hotel" (2014), directed by Wes Anderson. The film follows the adventures of Gustave H., a legendary concierge at a famous European hotel, and his trusted lobby boy, Zero, as they become embroiled in a theft and murder investigation. While the main plot of the film revolves around the mystery at the heart of the story, one of the film's many delights is its attention to detail and whimsical portrayal of the fictional Grand Budapest Hotel. The film showcases the hotel's opulent interior design, intricate architecture, and unique characters, all of which Gustave and Zero navigate with great charm and aplomb. In addition to the hotel itself, "The Grand Budapest Hotel" is also a love letter to European culture and history, with many scenes featuring picturesque landscapes and landmarks. The film also features a star-studded ensemble cast, including Ralph Fiennes, Tony Revolori, Tilda Swinton, Saoirse Ronan, and many others, all of whom bring their A-game to the screen.
